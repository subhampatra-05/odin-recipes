Paneer Butter Masala



Paneer Butter Masala is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a mildly spiced, buttery, tomato-based gravy. It's a popular vegetarian dish, often served with naan, roti, or rice. The dish is known for its smooth texture and slightly sweet flavor.

Ingredients

    For the gravy :
  • 2 tbsp butter (unsalted)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 medium onion (roughly chopped)
  • 3-4 medium tomatoes (roughly chopped)
  • 8-10 cashew nuts
  • 1 inch ginger
  • 3-4 garlic cloves
  • 1-2 green chilies (optional)
    • Spices :
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional, for balancing taste)
  • ½ tsp kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), crushed
    • Others requirements :
  • 200-250g paneer (cubed)
  • ¼ cup fresh cream
  • Water as needed
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Recipe

    step 1 : Prepare the base
  1. Heat 1 tbsp butter and 1 tbsp oil in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onions, garlic, ginger, green chilies, and sauté until golden.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cashews. Cook until tomatoes turn soft and mushy.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cashews. Cook until tomatoes turn soft and mushy.
    5. step 2 : Cook the gravy
  5. In the same pan, add 1 tbsp butter and cumin seeds + bay leaves.
  6. Pour the blended mixture into the pan and cook for 5-7 minutes on medium heat.
  7. Add chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until oil starts separating.
  8. Add a little water to adjust the consistency of the gravy.
    9. step 3 : Add paneer and finish
  9. Add a little water to adjust the consistency of the gravy.
  10. Add sugar, garam masala, crushed kasuri methi, and cream. Stir well.
  11. Simmer for 2-3 minutes and turn off the heat.
    12. step 4 : Garnish and serve
  12. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and an extra drizzle of cream or butter (optional).
